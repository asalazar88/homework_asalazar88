My Journey So Far

Rialto Raised

  • 2006

    Graduated From High School

    Born and raised in Rialto, CA I graduated high school in 2006 where I played on the freshman and JV soccer team, particiapted in Academic Decathlon, and wrote for my highschool newspaper. Major nerd alert, I know.

  • 2006. 2009

    UCLA

    Going into college, I knew that I wanted to study Comparative Literature. My favorite classes were exploring Mexican and German romanticism, existentialism and the Avante-garde. On the side I DJ'd for our online radio club, UCLAradio.com

  • 2011. 2013

    Two Years in Chicago

    The two years I spent living in Chicago opened my eyes to how different life was outside of California. The change of weather made me apprciate the spring and summer so much more without having to compromise the big-city life I love so much. Also, meeting different people taught how much friendlier the Midwest can be compared to the standoffishness of the LA crowds.

  • Present

    Livin' in LA

    Currently, I'm living in LA working in a non-profit housing organization and taking classes on computer programming (that kick ass) through General Assembly.
